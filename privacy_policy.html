Ciphora 隐私政策

1. 信息收集

Ciphora 应用（以下简称"本应用"）高度重视用户隐私保护。本应用不会收集任何个人身份信息。

2. 数据存储

2.1 本应用所有数据均存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

2.2 加密记录以加密形式存储在您的设备上，只有在您使用本应用时才会解密。

3. 数据安全

3.1 本应用使用行业标准的加密技术保护您的加密记录。

3.2 密钥保存在设备的安全区域(Keychain)中，受到系统保护。

4. 第三方访问

本应用不与任何第三方共享您的数据。

5. 政策更新

我们可能会更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本应用内和本网站上公布，继续使用本应用即表示您同意更新后的政策。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系 mark@u3dc.com。

Ciphora Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

The Ciphora application (hereinafter referred to as "the Application") highly values user privacy protection. This Application does not collect any personal identity information.

2. Data Storage

2.1 All data of this Application is stored locally on your device and will not be uploaded to any server.

2.2 Encryption records are stored in encrypted form on your device and are only decrypted when you use this Application.

3. Data Security

3.1 This Application uses industry-standard encryption technology to protect your encryption records.

3.2 Keys are stored in the secure area (Keychain) of the device and are protected by the system.

4. Third-Party Access

This Application does not share your data with any third parties.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated policy will be announced in this Application and on this website. Continued use of this Application indicates your agreement to the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact mark@u3dc.com.
